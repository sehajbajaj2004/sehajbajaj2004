## 🎮 About Me

I'm Sehaj, a final year Computer Science student at UPES Dehradun (CGPA 8.3). I sit at the intersection of software engineering, product thinking, and game development, and I like moving between the three rather than picking one.

Right now I'm a Product Intern at Leap Scholar in Bengaluru, where I work on onboarding funnels and growth experiments. Before that, I spent a summer at IIT Delhi's Department of Design building a VR training simulator for coal miners, in partnership with American Express.
